Legal & Regulations

We adhere to consumer protection laws, clear labeling, loot-box regulations, and robust return policies for damaged goods.

Consumer Protection Laws

Making sure that we are following consumer protection laws, for example following things like label laws, loot-box regulations, and return and refund policies for damaged products.

Legal / Design & Copyright Laws

Design and copyright issues: making sure we use unique designs for our cards to avoid being sued for infringements. Also making sure we comply with trademark laws.

Production Laws

Another legal issue is for sports: in order to put certain players on cards, we will need to be granted permission from the AFL or from other sports leagues.

Societal Trends

We embrace eco-friendly materials and recycled packaging to meet consumer values. Limited-edition drops and rare insert cards build hype and engage both casual and hardcore collectors.

Societal Attitudes

Being sustainable by using recycled paper and recycled materials to create our products. Also by using packaging which is made from recycled and recyclable materials.

Trends of Society

We won't create basic designs; we will make complex and innovative designs. We will achieve this by using trends such as having limited-edition releases that society values.

Society Values and Trends

We will make sure our products apply to a range of audiences such as making products for kids and die-hard collectors.

Economic Outlook

We adjust pricing and sourcing strategies in response to interest and tax rate fluctuations, ensuring affordability without compromising quality.

Interest / Tax Rates

When interest rates / tax rates increase, it will affect our business as we depend on discretionary spending. Conversely, when they decrease, customers have more disposable income to spend on our brand.

Business Planning

Along with this, when interest and tax rates increase, it will limit the amount we can spend to grow the business. When they decrease, it will allow us the opportunity to expand or grow our business.

Exchange Rates

When exchange rates change favorably for our company, we will be able to purchase more from our suppliers. However, when they go against us, we may have to consider changing where we source our materials or limit spending to maintain good supplier relationships.

Confidence of Business

When we have high confidence as a business, it will allow us to make more risky but future-benefiting decisions. When we have low confidence, we will be more cautious about where we spend our money.

Innovation & Technology

AI-driven material forecasting and customer loyalty systems optimize production. We explore augmented reality integrations and rapid prototyping to bring your card concepts to market faster.

Using Technology

We will start to use AI to prepare the amount of materials we need for our trading cards. Also, we will be able to use a rewards system through AI which calculates how much repeat customers can get off.

Using Technology & the Future

We will also look to use AI as a resource to help with customer service. This will help us save money and time not having to employ additional staff. For more complex issues, AI will help answer queries—eventually providing solutions to more advanced problems.

Design Helping & Ideas

For design ideas, we will be able to utilize AI to give us inspiration. This will allow us to save time while still having unique designs.

Global Reach

Our online storefront and distribution network spans multiple continents, making premium card products accessible worldwide.

Global Competitors

We will have competitors who manufacture overseas. To combat this, we will also aim to get some materials from overseas but produce them here.

Overseas Materials

With getting our materials from overseas but producing here, it will help reduce the chance of any people from different countries stealing our ideas.

Global Marketing

We can sell our products via online sales. This will allow us to reach a global market and increase sales potential.

Corporate Social Responsibility

We source materials from ethically-compliant suppliers, ensure fair labor practices, and invest in community sponsorships.

Social Responsibility

When sourcing our materials from other countries, we will make sure we are sourcing them from places where workers are paid fairly to produce the materials. This ensures we are acting in society’s best interest.

Environmental Considerations

We buy materials made from recycled paper, cardboard, and plastic to be more sustainable and reduce our carbon footprint.

Society’s Best Interests

We will sponsor local sports clubs—particularly youth Australian football clubs—to supply them with unique sports cards for their players and fans.

Customer Needs

We deliver high-quality printing options, protective accessories, and budget-friendly pricing tiers. Our flexible order volumes and discount programs reward repeat customers.

Customer Expectations

Customers expect a high-quality trading card. By offering varying releases that use special reflective materials or changing the thickness of the card, we can satisfy these needs. To do this, we have had to change some of our suppliers to be able to sell us these different materials.

Fair Pricing

Customers do not want to spend too much on our products. We will combat this by offering deals and promotions to maintain our reputation and encourage repeat business.

More Customer Expectations

Customers want to be able to display these cards and keep them in perfect condition—cards are collectibles, not items to bend or fold. To help with this, we have created a range of protective and display materials.

Competitive Advantage

Rather than competing on price alone, we focus on bespoke designs and premium materials. Our agility allows us to pivot quickly to market trends and maintain a unique product offering.

Quality of Products

We look at our competitors and, instead of being the cheapest option, we aim to be unique and create the highest-quality product possible. Focusing on quality often means we need to source higher-end materials and adjust our supplier relationships.

Discounts on Prices

Our competitors may slash prices to focus on volume, but we will keep a strong customer base by occasionally offering discounts—while still emphasizing our premium positioning.

Australian Made & Sustainability

We will have materials that are proudly “Australian made,” and some items will be fully sustainable to the environment. This will differentiate us from competitors and appeal to eco-conscious consumers.

Supply Chain

We partner with sustainable material suppliers and maintain local production facilities for quality control. Strategic supplier diversification reduces risk and ensures timely fulfillment.

Sourcing Sustainable Materials

We deeply consider the resources we use in our business to ensure they are sustainable for the environment. By making sure our materials are recycled or recyclable, we help care for the planet.

Fair Sourcing of Materials

We ensure our suppliers pay their workers fairly and maintain ethical practices. We will audit supply chains regularly to confirm compliance.

Where We Get Our Materials

We will carefully select countries from which to source our materials—such as choosing local Australian suppliers—to reduce transportation emissions and support the local economy.

Affinity & Industry Groups

We engage with environmental NGOs, trade associations, and unions to align our practices with societal best interests. Our memberships facilitate ongoing compliance and networking.

Environmental Groups

To address concerns from environmental lobby groups, we will plan many of our products to be fully sustainable and ecologically friendly.

Australia Made Groups

We will have all our products made within Australia. Having an “Australian Made” product targets many consumers who prefer locally made goods.

Trading Card Groups

We will target markets such as Comic-Con or small-market trading groups to advertise our products to communities like Pokémon or Yu-Gi-Oh enthusiasts.
